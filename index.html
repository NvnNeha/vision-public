Welcome

A school built on care, curiosity,
and quiet excellence.

For over two decades, Vision Public School has nurtured generations of students from Sikar and beyond. We believe a great school feels like a second home — rigorous in learning, warm in spirit, and rooted in the values that prepare children for life.

// Director

Mr. Anil Kumar Saini

A visionary educator with over 25 years in academic leadership, Mr. Saini founded Vision Public School with a simple promise: build a school where every child is known by name and helped to find their path.

Education isn't about filling a vessel — it's about lighting a fire.

// Principal

Mrs. Swati Saini

A passionate teacher-leader, Mrs. Saini brings warmth, structure, and high expectations to everything she does. She personally mentors faculty and meets parents with the care they'd hope for.
